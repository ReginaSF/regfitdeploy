What is RegFit?

RegFit is a tool for women who love training and are looking to enhance gains while understanding their bodies through Cycle Sync. It helps women adapt their workouts according to the four reproductive phases: menstruation, follicular, ovulation, and luteal.

1 Menstruation Phase

The bleeding phase marks day 1 of your cycle when estrogen and progesterone levels drop. Although the body experiences inflammation, this is a good time for HIIT workouts. Why? With lower estrogen and progesterone, the body absorbs carbohydrates better, making high-intensity workouts easier to perform.

2 Follicular Phase
A decrease in core temperature leads to better heat tolerance. This phase is ideal for increasing training intensity and adding more reps or weight in the gym.
3 Ovulation Phase

This phase lasts 12-24 hours, with rising estrogen and progesterone levels. It's a great time for heavy lifting and plyometrics due to high energy levels.

Best for high-intensity training

4 Luteal Phase

During this phase, inflammation and PMS symptoms may appear. Focus on technique rather than heavy lifting, but no need to pause training entirely. Energy levels may vary, so listen to your body.

Focus on technique

RegFit helps women understand the natural variations in their bodies throughout the month. It encourages conscious workouts that fit your cycle and energy levels without guilt or judgment.

Embrace your body and maximize performance
